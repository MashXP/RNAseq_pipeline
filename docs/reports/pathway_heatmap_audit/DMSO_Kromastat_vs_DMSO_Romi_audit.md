# STATUS: DEPRECATED / NOT WORTH AUDITING

### Rationale
This audit has been discontinued for the following reasons:
1. **Redundant Baseline Comparison**: The comparison focuses on `DMSO_Kromastat` vs. `DMSO_Romi`, which effectively measures batch-to-batch variation between control groups rather than biological treatment effects.
2. **High Background Noise**: The general expression landscape across these controls is visually "messy" and does not yield clear, reproducible pathway signatures.
3. **Lack of Biological Significance**: Findings in this comparison do not contribute to the understanding of Romidepsin or Kromastat efficacy and are considered background variance.
